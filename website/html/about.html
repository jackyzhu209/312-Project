About Us

Teamporium is a networking site designed to allow users to create listings for projects and ideas they plan to create, and find others who would like to join on with them.
What kinds of projects? Anything you can think of! Listing posters can specify project descriptions, pay rate, and any other info they wish to.
Don't have an idea in mind? Then feel free to browse through the listings and message a poster if you want to inquire more.
